Step 3 of 4

The properties.

Enter realistic shortlist numbers. Just the price, lease, and loan type are enough to start. Fine-tune the rest if you want.

What about BTO? This tool focuses on resale HDB and private property because those are decisions you can act on now with known prices. BTO flats are not modeled because the timeline depends on a ballot, and waiting times can range from 3 to 6 years. If you are considering BTO, note that first-timer couples can receive CPF housing grants of up to S$80,000 (Enhanced CPF Housing Grant + Family Grant), which can significantly reduce the effective price. You can use this tool by entering the BTO price minus applicable grants as the 'Resale HDB' price, and setting appreciation conservatively since newer BTO flats often take years to catch up to resale market levels.
